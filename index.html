Brief History

Welcome to our school

THINK BIG SCHOOL : Our school, think Big Academy started in the year 2017 at no 33 Uniport Road Omuahunwo Aluu Rivers State with about 8 pupils now we have over 100 pupils in our school. We believe in every child and are building leaders for a sustainable future while providing an exciting,high quality and holistic education.
